# Privacy Policy - JLS Finance Suite

**Effective Date:** December 27, 2025

## Introduction
**JLS Finance Suite** is a financial record management and accounting application developed by **Lavneet Rathi**.
This app **does not provide loans, financial products, or financial advice**.
It is designed only to help users **record, manage, and view their own financial and loan-related data**.

---

## Data We Collect
The app collects only the data that **users voluntarily enter**, such as:
* Name
* Phone Number
* Customer details
* Loan records (amount, EMI dates, payment status)
* Accounting entries

⚠️ **Important:**
We do **not** collect bank credentials, OTPs, Aadhaar, PAN, or card details.

---

## Purpose of Data Collection
Data is collected **only to provide core app functionality**, including:
* Accounting management
* Loan record tracking
* EMI schedules
* Payment history
* Optional reminders initiated by the user

---

## Loan Disclaimer
This application:
* ❌ Does NOT provide loans
* ❌ Does NOT approve or reject loans
* ❌ Does NOT act as a lender, NBFC, or financial institution

All loan-related data is **user-maintained for record-keeping purposes only**.

---

## Data Storage & Security
* Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices
* Data is encrypted during transmission
* We do not sell or share user data with third parties
