Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 01, 2026

At JLS BillBook, we prioritize the trust regarding your business data. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines exactly how we handle data within our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Credentials
We collect your Email Address and Password (encrypted) to create and secure your unique account.
1.2 Profile Details
Your Display Name and Phone Number are collected to personalize your profile and invoices.
1.3 Business Profile
Company Name, Address, GSTIN, and Logo are collected to generate professional invoices on your behalf.
1.4 Customer Directory
Names, Phone Numbers, and Addresses of your customers that YOU manually enter for your records.
1.5 Financial Transactions
Details of sales, receipts, expenses, and payments (Dates, Amounts, Items) entered by you.
1.6 Device Metadata
We collect Device Model, OS Version, and App Version to debug crashes and optimize performance.
1.7 IP Address
Collected automatically by our cloud providers (Firebase) for security and fraud prevention.
1.8 User Content
Images of receipts, products, or signatures uploaded by you to attach to records.
1.9 AI Processing Data
When you voluntarily use AI features (like "AI Scan"), the image data is temporarily sent to third-party AI providers (Google Gemini) for processing. We do not store this data for AI training.
1.10 Cloud Backup Data (Optional)
If you choose to enable Google Drive cloud backup, your business data (invoices, customers, products, payments, expenses, and company profile) will be backed up to YOUR OWN Google Drive account. We DO NOT have access to your Google Drive. The backup files are encrypted and stored in a folder called "BillBook Backups" in your Drive. You maintain full control and can disconnect or delete backups anytime. This feature requires your explicit consent via Google OAuth authentication.

2. Device Permissions

2.1 INTERNET
Mandatory. Required to sync your data with our secure cloud database so you can access it anywhere.
2.2 CAMERA
Optional. Used to take photos of physical bills or products directly within the app.
2.3 WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Optional. Used to save generated PDF invoices and reports to your device's downloads folder.
2.4 VIBRATE
Used to provide haptic feedback when you successfully save a record or delete an item.

3. How We Use Data

3.1 Service Provision
To provide the core billing, invoice generation, and ledger management features.
3.2 Authentication
To verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access to your business books.
3.3 Customer Support
To assist you when you contact us (we only access data with your explicit permission).
3.4 Analytics
To understand which features are used most, using aggregated anonymous data.
3.5 Security
To detect suspicious login attempts or bulk data scraping activities.
3.6 AI Features
If you enable AI features by providing your API key, we process your bill images to extract text. This processing happens via secure API calls to your chosen provider.
3.7 Cloud Backup (Optional)
If you opt-in to Google Drive backup, your data is securely stored in YOUR Google Drive account (not our servers). We use Google Drive API only to create, list, restore, and delete backup files. We NEVER access your personal Drive files or folders beyond the dedicated "BillBook Backups" folder. All backup operations require your active Google account authentication.

4. Your Rights & Control

4.1 Right to Access
You can view all your stored invoices, customers, and profile data directly within the app.
4.2 Right to Rectify
You can edit any customer or profile detail instantly via the Edit options.
4.3 Right to Export
You can download invoices as PDF or export full data as JSON from Settings.
4.4 Right to Delete Account
You may permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings.
4.5 Right to Manage Cloud Backups
You have complete control over cloud backups: (a) Connect or disconnect Google Drive anytime, (b) Create manual backups whenever needed, (c) Set automatic backup frequency (manual, daily, weekly), (d) View all your backups with creation dates, (e) Restore from any backup, (f) Delete individual backups or all backups, (g) Revoke app access via Google Account settings. Your backups remain in YOUR Google Drive even after disconnecting from the app.
