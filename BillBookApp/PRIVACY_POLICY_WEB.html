Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 01, 2026

At JLS BillBook, we prioritize the trust regarding your business data. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines exactly how we handle the data points and interactions within our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Credentials: We collect your Email Address and Password (encrypted) to create and secure your unique account.
  • Profile Details: Your Display Name and Phone Number are collected to personalize your profile and invoices.
  • Business Profile: Company Name, Address, GSTIN, and Logo are collected to generate professional invoices on your behalf.
  • Customer Directory: Names, Phone Numbers, and Addresses of your customers that YOU manually enter for your records.
  • Financial Transactions: Details of sales, receipts, expenses, and payments (Dates, Amounts, Items) entered by you.
  • Device Metadata: We collect Device Model, OS Version, and App Version to debug crashes and optimize performance.
  • IP Address: Collected automatically by our cloud providers (Firebase) for security and fraud prevention.
  • User Content: Images of receipts, products, or signatures uploaded by you to attach to records.

2. Device Permissions

  • INTERNET: Mandatory. Required to sync your data with our secure cloud database so you can access it anywhere.
  • CAMERA: Optional. Used to take photos of physical bills or products directly within the app.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Optional. Used to save generated PDF invoices and reports to your device's downloads folder.
  • READ_CONTACTS: Optional. We DO NOT upload your contacts. This permission is used ONLY locally on your device to help you autofill customer details (Name/Phone) when creating a new party.
  • VIBRATE: Used to provide haptic feedback when you successfully save a record or delete an item.

3. How We Use Data

  • Service Provision: To provide the core billing, invoice generation, and ledger management features.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access to your business books.
  • Customer Support: To assist you when you contact us (we only access data with your explicit permission).
  • Analytics: To understand which features are used most, using aggregated anonymous data.
  • Security: To detect suspicious login attempts or bulk data scraping activities.

4. Your Rights & Control

  • Right to Access: You can view all your stored invoices, customers, and profile data directly within the app.
  • Right to Rectify: You can edit any customer or profile detail instantly via the Edit options.
  • Right to Export: You can download invoices as PDF or export full data as JSON from Settings.
  • Right to Delete Account: You may permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings > Delete Account. This action is irreversible.
