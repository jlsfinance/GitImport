# PRIVACY POLICY (INDIA-CENTRIC COMPLIANCE)
Last Updated: December 25, 2024

### 1. INTRODUCTION
Welcome to BillBook ("Application"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the **Information Technology Act, 2000**, the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023**, and other applicable laws of India.

### 2. DATA WE COLLECT
- **User Provided Data**: Name, Phone Number, Email, and Business Details provided during registration.
- **Transactional Data**: Invoices, customer names, and amount details stored within the app.
- **Device Permissions**: 
    - **Contacts**: We request access to your contacts to allow you to save customer details directly to your phone's address book.
    - **Storage**: To save generated PDF invoices.

### 3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
- To provide invoicing and billing services.
- To sync data across your devices via Firebase (if enabled).
- **Contact Syncing**: We use the native Android Contacts API to save customer information locally on your device. We do not upload your contact list to our servers unless specifically requested for backup.

### 4. DATA SECURITY
We implement industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and SSL/TLS for data in transit. 

### 5. YOUR RIGHTS
Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to:
- Access and correct your information.
- Withdraw consent at any time.
- Request deletion of your account/data.
